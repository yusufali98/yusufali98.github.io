Hi! I am a PhD student at the School of Interactive Computing, Georgia Tech working with <a href='https://faculty.cc.gatech.edu/~zk15/'> Prof. Zsolt Kira </a>. My research focuses on <strong>improving foundation models at test time</strong> without relying on large-scale data collection, heavy fine-tuning, or sacrificing general model capabilities in diverse application domains. Most recently, I have explored this problem in the context of <a href='https://yusufali98.github.io/eve-vla-recovery/'>embodied VLA models</a>. I have also studied the capabilities and limitations of frontier multimodal LLMs in settings that require <a href='https://findingdory-benchmark.github.io/'>long-horizon memory</a> and robust performance in <a href='https://ogmenrobotics.github.io/K9Bench/#'>low-data domains</a>. My current work investigates how minimal and targeted test-time updates, particularly through reinforcement learning, can improve VLA performance and multimodal video reasoning. Please reach out if you would like to discuss ideas or collaborate !
